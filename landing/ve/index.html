What's different.

Determinism

Same inputs, same outputs, every time. VAs producing the same kind of clip across a client roster get the same look without thinking about it.

Real timeline

The agent does the heavy lift. The VA scrubs, marks in and out, drags clips around. Approval gates pause at every stage that needs human eyes.

Hard-stop budget

Each client gets a cap. We enforce it. No surprise overrun on a Friday afternoon when the operations lead is at brunch.
