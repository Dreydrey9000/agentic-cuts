What's different.

Determinism

Every tool takes a seed. Same prompt + seed + model version = byte-identical output, cached on disk. Re-runs are free. The opposite of every closed video tool that varies frame-level decisions every time you click render.

Real timeline

Not chat-only. Mosaic and Cardboard admit chat is the wrong UX for video. We ship a timeline you can scrub, drag, and override. The agent edits the JSON. You supervise the gestures.

Hard-stop budget

OpenMontage warns when you blow past your cap. We kill the run. CAP mode raises a real exception. No surprise bills. No zombie renders chewing API credit at three in the morning.
